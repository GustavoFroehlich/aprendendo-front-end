About Us:

Where we came from:

In 2008, in the pulsating heart of New York City, a unique gastronomic experience emerged that challenged conventions and expanded culinary horizons: SaborBite. Founded in the midst of the digital era's fervor, this innovative burger joint dove headfirst into the realm of bits and bytes, captivating the palates and minds of New Yorkers in an electrifying manner.

What is our goal:

With its bold design reminiscent of a vintage computing environment, ByteBurger transported its patrons on a journey through time, celebrating digital pop culture. The decor, filled with references to retro game icons and pixelated elements, created a one-of-a-kind atmosphere where each corner seemed like a pixel of nostalgia.

Our amazing burgers

But the true star, of course, was the burgers. The menu paid homage to cybernetic creativity, featuring names like "Binary Beef Burger," "Pixel Pepper Jack," and "RAM Ranch Burger." Each bite was a fusion of unexpected flavors, as if the ingredients were encoded to surprise taste buds. Customers could customize their orders through an interactive digital system at each table, further amplifying the interactive experience.
